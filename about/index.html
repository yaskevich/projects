Alyaxey Yaskevich – Science

Science of data and meanings

    Relation between form and meaning it bears is what I investigate.

    My scientific interests are morphology (word formation), language variation and change, and cognitive approaches to language study. Particularly, my attention is attracted to non-standard types of language – slang, informal language, and so called computer mediated communication (CMC) as a manifestation of written colloquial form of speech. On the other hand, I'm interested in language attitude issues. Except of general questions, I'm deeply involved in the problems of the current linguistic situation in Belarus. Also I'm very fond of computer-assisted language studies (corpus linguistics, NLP, e-lexicography).

    Another (mostly linguistic) about-me in Belarusian is here: www.philology.by/yaskevich
